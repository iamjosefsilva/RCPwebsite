How To Make Strawberry Macarons

These are the best macarons you will ever have! These macarons are flavorfull and deliciuos!

Ingredients Needed

  • 1/2 cup dried strawberries
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tarter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Red/Pink food coloring

1. First combine the almond and powdered sugar.

2. Combine egg whites, cream of tarter, and sugar into a bowl. Then beat it until stiff peaks.

3. Carefully fold the two mixtures together and incorperate the food coloring and strawberries.

4.Put the batter into apiping bag and pipe on to a lines paper tray. You can make any shaped macarons!

5. Let the macarons sit for 30 minutes until a thin film is created. To test if the macarons are ready simplay touch the macarons. If the batter doesnt stick to your finger they are ready to be baked!

6. Bake the macarons in a 300deg (150 C) preheated oven for 17 minutes. After they are done baking let them cool for about 30 minutes.
